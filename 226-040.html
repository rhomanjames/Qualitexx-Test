Window Perf: Perforated Window Vinyl with Do-All Liner

226-040 | Laird Item #89582


A series of economy grade composite PVC perforated solutions delivers vivid graphics in a one-way film that is perfect for windows, doors, vehicles or anywhere you want your message to come to life. Qualitexx provides a single source solution to all your window and door graphic needs, with multiple options of transparency and boldness.

Features and Benefits
  • High resolution graphics on the outside, while retaining exterior viewing from the inside
  • Reduces heat and glare from the sun
  • Maintains exterior graphic quality for up to one year
  • Step-by-step instructions make application easy
