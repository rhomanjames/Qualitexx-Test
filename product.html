Gloss White 2.0 mil Premium High Performance Cast Vinyl

221-800 | Laird Item #92089


PROCAST 221-800 from Laird Plastics is a gloss white flexible CAST vinyl. This premium high-performance, flexible cast vinyl is ideal for application to irregular surfaces, suitable for vehicle wraps, bus graphics, fleet graphics and more.

Features and Benefits

  • Grayback adhesive
  • Up to 7 years outdoors
